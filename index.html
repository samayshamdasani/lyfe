11 | 06 | 12 | Eleven years old

MAKING YOUTUBE VIDEOS

This was such a big event in my life because my dad had just bought an iMac for the family - something that further pursued my interest in technology. Every day after school, I began to sit down and watch guys like MKBHD, Austin Evans, and Jonathan Morrision on YouTube and constantly learning about the latest software, phones, tablets, laptops, and cameras (and basically anything related to the vast world of technology). That's when I was inspired to start my own channel. My first video was an unboxing of my mom's iPhone 5 and I remember how I forced my mom to hold the camera while I narrarated the specs of the phone. When I hit 100 subscribers and started getting small products like cases and speakers from companies to review, I felt pretty important. To this day, I enjoy creating videos and showing them to the world (and I hope I still will).
